About us!

Good Food Mood is a website to help you look for recipes outside what you normally would try and make!
With an easy to use search engine with thousands of recipes from all over the world. Even if you have allergies or special dietary constrictions its super simple to find something that suits you!

Our vision

Our vision is to make everyone love the kitchen and making their own food, spending time in the kitchen should be a creative and fun experience and we want to help make it so! By providing recipes from all over the world, and no mather what you like to eat we have something for everyone.

Narrowing down options!

In a stressful week with work,kids life in general getting in the way, making it hard to decide what to have for dinner! Or if you want to be creative but don't know where to start! Try our cuisine picker! It gives you a place to start by just a click of a button it gives you a type of cuisine, which you can use in the search engine to find something you would like to make.

Hosting a dinner party!

Has never been easier, you can use the search engine for everything from drinks, starters, main course even deserts. or baked goods. Fun recipes for drinks both with and without alcohol for a fun night with the people you love. Children's birthday party? No problem, fun cakes, fun food, all fun!
